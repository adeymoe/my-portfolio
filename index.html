FUN FACT!!

Did you know that the Python programming language, was named after the British comedy series "Monty Python's Flying Circus"? Guido van Rossum, the creator of Python, wanted a name that was short, unique, and slightly mysterious. This playful origin reflects Python's ethos of making coding more fun and accessible. Today, Python's combination of simplicity and powerful capabilities makes it a favorite tool for everything!
